W15: NLP for Medical Conversations (NLPMC)

Parminder Bhatia, Chaitanya Shivade, Mona Diab, Byron Wallace, Rashmi Gangadharaiah, Nan Du, Izhak Shafran, Steven Lin

Description Schedule External Website
Live Session: Jul 10 (16:00-01:00 GMT)
NLP for Medical Conversations aims to Build the clinical decision support systems as cognitive assistants by bridging the gap between NLP research and medical practice.
