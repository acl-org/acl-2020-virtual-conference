W4: Workshop on Biomedical Natural Language Processing (BioNLP)

Dina Demner-Fushman, Kevin Cohen, Sophia Ananiadou, Jun'ichi Tsujii

Description Schedule External Website
Live Session: Jul 9 (15:30-01:00 GMT)
NLP can help improve health and BioNLP is the best venue for biomedical & clinical language processing researchers to learn how.
