W20: NLP COVID-19 Workshop (NLPCovid)

Karin Verspoor, Kevin Cohen, Mark Dredze, Emilio Ferrara, Jonathan May, Robert Munro, Cecile Paris, Byron Wallace

Description Schedule External Website
Live Session: Jul 10 (00:30-04:30 GMT)
NLPCovid invite submissions related to any aspect of natural language processing (NLP) applied to combat the COVID-19 pandemic, in an open, rapid review format that will allow this active and late-breaking research to be shared and discussed.
