W16: E-Commerce and NLP (ECNLP)

Shervin Malmasi, Eugene Agichtein, Surya Kallumadi, Oleg Rokhlenko, Nicola Ueffing, Ido Guy

Description Schedule External Website
Live Session: Jul 10 (12:30-21:15 GMT)
The Third Workshop on e-Commerce and NLP focuses on NLP for e-Commerce and online shopping applications. Shared tasks on classification and Question Answering are included.
