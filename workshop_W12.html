W12: Workshop on Neural Generation and Translation (WNGT)

Alexandra Birch, Graham Neubig, Andrew Finch, Hiroaki Hayashi, Kenneth Heafield, Ioannis Konstas, Yusuke Oda, Xian Li

Description Schedule External Website
Live Session: Jul 10 (15:30-00:00 GMT)
The 4th Workshop on Neural Generation and Translation provides a forum for research in applications of neural models to language generation and translation tasks.
