W19: The Second Grand-Challenge and Workshop on Human Multimodal Language (Challenge-HML)

AmirAli Bagher Zadeh, Louis-Philippe Morency, Paul Pu Liang, Soujanya Poria, Ying Shen

Description Schedule External Website
Live Session: Jul 10 (13:00-21:40 GMT)
The Second Grand-Challenge and Workshop on Human Multimodal Language promotes the growth of modeling multimodal language (language, vision, acoustic) as a fundamental research area in NLP.
